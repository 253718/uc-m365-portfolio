## Portfolio — Delivery Architect — Teams Voice (regulated environments)

This public repository demonstrates how Teams Voice and adjacent Microsoft 365 delivery work can be presented in a mature, evidence-oriented way **without exposing private operational tooling**.

Its current primary path is feed-driven and focuses on technical review artefacts, DAT snippets, evidence packs, and handover-supporting outputs generated from a validated read-only feed.

## What is visible here

- consumer-side feed ingestion
- feed-driven review and evidence outputs
- DAT snippet generation
- evidence packs
- delivery templates, case studies, ADRs, and sample materials

## Why the 3-repo split improves the public repo

The split does not weaken the portfolio.
It clarifies responsibilities:

- producer repo collects facts
- change repo performs controlled mutations privately
- this public repo turns facts into handover-supporting, review-ready artefacts

That makes the public signal stronger:

- clearer operating model
- stronger read-only posture
- better recruiter-facing coherence
- preserved technical breadth
