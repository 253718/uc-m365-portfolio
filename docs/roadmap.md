## Roadmap

This roadmap outlines illustrative evolution axes for the public consumer repository.

It focuses on strengthening technical review value, evidence quality, and delivery coherence rather than expanding into a full architecture dossier.

## Near-term focus

- richer DAT snippet coverage across routing, dial plans, normalization rules, and service-entry patterns
- stronger premium sample fixtures illustrating realistic multi-operator and multi-country Teams Phone scenarios
- case-study-driven documentation that links feed fixtures, review outputs, and handover-supporting artefacts

## Mid-term focus

- expanded normalized feed domains beyond Teams Phone
- stronger correlation between inventories, validation, reviewer outputs, and review findings
- feed-driven review and evidence patterns for adjacent voice infrastructure domains such as SBC / AudioCodes
- more public examples of delivery-to-run industrialization in regulated and service-critical voice contexts

## Constant principle

The goal is **not** to make the portfolio smaller.
The goal is to keep it broad while improving architectural coherence.
