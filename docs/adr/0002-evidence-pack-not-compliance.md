## ADR-0002 — Evidence Pack is Not Compliance

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-01-15

### Context

Evidence artefacts are frequently confused with compliance claims in regulated environments.

### Decision

Evidence packs produced by this portfolio are **fact-only, read-only artefact collections**.
They support audits, handovers, and reviews, but they do not certify compliance.

### Consequences

- integrity manifests and timestamps matter
- the public repo does not claim control coverage or certification
- customer governance and sign-off remain out of scope
